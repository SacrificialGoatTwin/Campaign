==========================
CHAPTER 3 — FULL SEQUENTIAL BREAKDOWN
==========================

TIME & SETTING
- Date: 21/04/1427
- Start Time: ~15:36
- Route: Westbridge → Harrowfen
- Initial Location: Owned wagon on the road outside Westbridge
- End Location: Inn room in Harrowfen, night rain

----------------------------------
CORE PARTY
----------------------------------
- Jayson Goodwin / Shel’mekai’Vuran-Tah’aan (PC, Kitsune, power-suppressed, Spark active)
- Kestrel (C-rank adventurer, human/veil-marked, later reveals true name Thrennael)
- Liri (beastkin child rescued from traffickers)

----------------------------------
MAJOR EVENTS (SEQUENTIAL)
----------------------------------

1) ROAD ESCORT & INITIAL SUSPICION
- Jayson, Kestrel, and Liri travel in a legally claimed wagon back toward Harrowfen.
- Three Guild riders audit the wagon.
- Jayson examines the writ identifying him as collaborating witness and escort.
- Notices one rider behaving unnaturally (twitchy, scanning hedgerows).

2) AMBUSH REVEAL & WAGON SEPARATION
- Jayson probes the hedgerow with thrown stones.
- Hidden attackers are revealed.
- Wagon bolts; Kestrel rides ahead with Liri.
- Jayson stays behind to intercept attackers and guards.

3) ROAD COMBAT (NON-MAGICAL, HIGH SPEED)
- Jayson incapacitates ambushers with thrown stones and pursuit.
- One ambusher is captured alive by guards.
- Jayson detects wagon diversion and sprints after it at full Kitsune speed.

4) CAVE ASSAULT & RESCUE
- Wagon stopped by attackers near a cliffside cave.
- Jayson arrives mid-assault.
- Lethal force used against attackers threatening Kestrel and Liri.
- Climbs cliff face using claws and tails.
- Neutralizes remaining captors inside the cave.
- Cuts Liri free.
- Bandages Kestrel manually.
- Guilt and emotional collapse over nearly losing them.

5) FINAL ENCOUNTER & DETERRENCE
- Additional hostile figure revealed.
- Jayson deliberately spares one survivor.
- Makes clear declaration: future encounters will not be survived.
- Intentional choice to create deterrent witness rather than silent slaughter.

6) GUILD ARRIVAL & AFTERMATH
- Guild troops arrive.
- Healers stabilize Kestrel.
- Jayson openly acknowledges killing humans.
- Marshal confirms context matters; no immediate punishment.
- Guild summons Jayson later to discuss next steps.

7) RETURN TO HARROWFEN
- Quiet ride back.
- Emotional shame and restraint from Jayson.
- Wagon returned.
- Escort to inn.
- Jayson goes to blacksmith to honor promise.

8) BLACKSMITH & WEAPON COMPLETION
- Jayson apologizes for delay.
- Finishes forging katana.
- Engraves origami fox above handguard.
- Pays blacksmith respectfully.
- Weapon concealed in coat.

9) INN — EVENING REPRIEVE
- Reunion with Kestrel and Liri.
- Food, rest, calm.
- Jayson proposes:
  - Guild leveling plaque
  - Dungeon work
  - Travel to capital to study magic
- Asks Kestrel to accompany him.
- Kestrel agrees.
- Mutual emotional grounding.

10) GUILD SUMMONS (NEXT DAY)
- Jayson receives Guild letter.
- Attends meeting promptly with Kestrel and Liri.
- Explains reasoning behind sparing survivor.
- Guild accepts explanation.
- Status remains monitored but cooperative.

11) MARKET DAY & CIVILIAN LIFE
- Market visit.
- Gifts Kestrel engraved bracers.
- Buys Liri toys and bread.
- Light-hearted bonding.
- Reveals deep homesickness for his wives.

12) FLEAS — SPIRIT REVEAL
- Jayson checks fifth tail shrine-style.
- Fox spirit kit “Fleas” emerges.
- Fleas can now speak (new development).
- Playful physical scuffle.
- Confirms Fleas still bound to his fifth tail.
- Agreement: Fleas will respond to calls even if hidden.

13) PHYSICAL HEALING
- Jayson diagnoses Kestrel’s shoulder.
- Performs non-magical tendon realignment.
- Painful but effective.
- Demonstrates old-world skill over magic reliance.

14) LOWER MARKET INN — HOSTILITY
- Party unknowingly enters hostile, beastkin-hating district.
- Verbal harassment escalates.
- Jayson deliberately redirects conflict outside.
- Non-lethal martial engagement with human aggressor.
- Public de-escalation without bloodshed.
- Makes statement about generational hate without preaching.

15) NIGHT RAIN & REFLECTION
- Party separates for sleep.
- Jayson sits alone watching rain.
- Reflects on loss of wives, past arrogance, growth.
- Carves stone with spiral and portraits of his seven wives.
- Chapter ends in quiet grief and resolve.
