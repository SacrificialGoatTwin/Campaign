📜 CHAPTER 1 — FULL SEQUENTIAL BREAKDOWN
---
🌅 ARRIVAL & INITIAL STATE
 * Jayson awakens alone in an ancient forest on Eryndor after being forcibly displaced from Tsunejima.
 * Immediately discovers:
   * Magic and flight are severely restricted or unavailable.
   * Physical conditioning remains, but reserves are drained.
 * Senses his wives are alive but very distant.
 * Encounters the God of this world in a liminal state.
   * Offered three “mercies.”
   * Rejects Shape and other options.
   * Accepts Spark (slow, growth-based power tied to sacrifice and endurance).
   * Demands his four accompanying wives be kept together, even if far away.
   * God complies; their signatures converge, then vanish to extreme distance.
---
🐾 FIRST THREAT CONTACT (LISTENERS)
 * Jayson detects sound-based predators in the forest.
 * Uses thrown stones to create moving sound illusions.
 * Confirms:
   * Creatures hunt by sound.
   * Are fast, jointed, insectile.
 * Encounters Corren, a human traveler.
   * They mutually avoid Listeners.
   * Corren guides Jayson to a road and toward civilization.
---
🏙️ HARROWFEN ARRIVAL & GUILD REGISTRATION
 * Jayson reaches Harrowfen.
 * Learns:
   * Beastfolk are tolerated but restricted.
   * Registration is required for legality.
 * Registers at the Adventurers’ Guild without papers.
 * Accepts first jobs due to lack of coin.
---
🛡️ EARLY JOBS & REPUTATION BUILDING
 * Listener Report:
   * Describes creatures accurately to the Guild.
 * Guard Rescue:
   * Finds two guards captured by Listeners.
   * Uses all nine tails to extract them.
   * Conceals identity and requests silence.
 * Rat Problem – Baker’s Cellar:
   * Discovers “rats” are ratkin refugees.
   * Negotiates a winter survival pact:
     * Food + shelter in exchange for labor.
     * Keeps situation hidden.
 * Additional Ratkin Eviction (Tanner’s Cellar):
   * Warns a second brood to flee before Guild action escalates.
   * Provides hides for warmth.
   * Seals entry to prevent discovery.
 * Gains a reputation for:
   * Mercy.
   * Effectiveness.
   * Discretion.
---
🏥 CIVILIAN INTERVENTION
 * Stops a street thief (Tomas).
 * Discovers theft motivated by sister’s illness.
 * Personally carries the girl to a healer.
 * Pays for treatment.
 * Forces accountability and responsibility from Tomas.
 * Establishes trust with local healer.
---
🚨 KIDNAPPING & VAELIR INVOLVEMENT
 * Meets Lyra, a Vaelir woman displaced by a spatial anomaly.
 * Learns:
   * Her sister Mara Valen went missing shortly before Lyra’s reappearance.
 * Investigates her sister’s home.
 * Captures an infiltrator posing as a dock worker.
 * Discovers:
   * Lyra and her sister were targeted deliberately.
   * Jayson is the real target.
 * Reports kidnapping to Westbridge Guild branch.
 * Obtains a legal writ.
 * Executes lawful rescue at Warehouse 9.
 * Mara recovered alive.
 * Guild and city authorities take over investigation.
 * Confirms systemic prejudice against Vaelir.
---
🏚️ RUINS & WATCHER ENCOUNTER
 * Partners with Kestrel Thorne, C-rank adventurer.
 * Investigates sealed ruins.
 * Discovers:
   * Basement barred from the inside.
   * Leads into unauthorized tunnel system.
 * Encounter Watcher-class entity.
   * Identifies Jayson as a “Threshold.”
   * Non-hostile but territorial.
 * Withdraws to avoid escalation.
 * Marshal later confirms area was not authorized.
---
⛏️ QUARRY & LISTENER EXPLOITATION
 * With Kestrel, investigates quarry collapse.
 * Uses sound misdirection to avoid Listeners.
 * Discovers:
   * Evidence of Listeners being captured and tested.
   * Harnesses, coin caches, research materials.
 * Executes rapid extraction through collapsed culvert.
 * Splits loot 60/40 in Kestrel’s favor.
 * Submits findings to the Guild.
 * Accidentally reveals kitsune ears publicly.
---
🌃 NIGHT WATCH & VAMPIRES
 * Assigned warehouse night watch.
 * Detects decoy theft.
 * Intercepts real infiltration.
 * Confronts:
   * A pureborn vampire woman.
   * Her turned human husband.
 * Learns:
   * Dangerous crate exists but is not theirs.
   * Multiple factions are interested.
 * Allows them to leave after confirming threat.
 * Captures an unrelated thief.
---
👁️ SURVEILLANCE ESCALATION
 * Discovers he is being:
   * Observed.
   * Indexed.
   * Evaluated as an anomaly.
 * Confronts observers near the inn.
 * Learns:
   * Alchemical fire (napalm-like) prepared as contingency.
   * Containment was discussed.
 * Nearly kills them.
 * Kestrel intervenes, grounding him with a breathing cue.
 * Jayson chooses restraint.
 * Withdraws to inn.
---
🌙 CHAPTER CLOSE
 * Locks himself into his room.
 * Emotionally isolated.
 * No confirmation of wives’ safety.
 * Fully aware:
   * He is now known.
   * He is being studied.
   * Restraint is the only reason blood has not been spilled.
