CHAPTER 3 — FULL SUMMARY
(Tags [000001] → [000174])

DATE & TIMEFRAME
- Date: 21–22/04/1427 (Eryndor calendar)
- Time progression: Afternoon → Evening → Night
- Weather: Clear afternoon shifting into winter rain by evening

LOCATION OVERVIEW
- Westbridge outskirts → Road to Harrowfen
- Harrowfen (Lower Market District, Red Lantern Row)
- Harrowfen river shrine
- Harrowfen Rainward Inn (North Quarter)

PARTY STATUS
- Jayson (PC): Kitsune, restrained godhood (“Spark”), operating without overt magic
- Kestrel: Companion, injured earlier (shoulder tendon), later corrected manually
- Liri: Rescued beastkin child, emotionally attached to Jayson, under his protection

────────────────────────
1) RETURN FROM WESTBRIDGE & ROAD INCIDENT
- Jayson, Kestrel, and Liri travel back toward Harrowfen in their wagon.
- Guild riders audit them; Jayson presents a valid writ identifying him as:
  - Collaborating witness
  - Authorized escort for Liri
- Jayson notices suspicious behavior from a third rider watching the hedgerow.
- He tests the area with thrown stones, revealing an ambush.
- The horse panics and bolts with the wagon; Kestrel rides after it with Liri.
- Jayson stays behind and engages both guards and ambushers:
  - Disables multiple attackers with precision stone throws.
  - One guard already has another ambusher detained.
- Jayson chases the wagon on foot at full speed.

────────────────────────
2) WAGON AMBUSH & ESCALATION
- Jayson reaches the stopped wagon; Kestrel and Liri have been taken.
- He attacks immediately:
  - Lethal force against multiple attackers (throats crushed, bodies disabled).
  - Climbs a cliff face at extreme speed to reach Liri’s cries.
- In a cave above:
  - Kestrel is bound and injured.
  - Liri is restrained.
- Jayson kills all visible attackers inside the cave.
- One final hostile reveals themselves; Jayson intimidates but spares them deliberately.
  - He releases them to spread word of what he is capable of.
- Jayson frees Liri, bandages Kestrel, and shows deep remorse.
- Guild troops arrive shortly after.

────────────────────────
3) GUILD RESPONSE & CONSEQUENCES
- Guild healers magically heal Kestrel’s wounds (pain remains).
- Jayson openly admits to killing humans.
- The Marshal acknowledges the complexity and risk.
- No immediate punishment; matter is under review.
- Jayson explains why he spared one enemy:
  - To discourage open attacks and force fear-driven caution instead.
- Guild accepts his reasoning.
- Jayson states future plans:
  - Continue guild work
  - Gain a plaque to level up
  - Travel to the capital to study magic
- Guild summons Jayson for follow-up discussion later (7-day window).

────────────────────────
4) RETURN TO HARROWFEN & BLACKSMITH
- Jayson escorts Kestrel and Liri to an inn.
- He goes alone to the blacksmith he promised to meet.
- Completes forging his katana:
  - Polishing
  - Handle and twine fitted
  - Scabbard finished
  - Engraves a small origami fox above the handguard
- Pays the blacksmith and returns to the inn.

────────────────────────
5) INN NIGHT & DECISIONS
- Reunites with Kestrel and Liri.
- Shares stew; Liri sleeps.
- Jayson apologizes to Kestrel for nearly getting her killed.
- He proposes:
  - Advancing via guild plaques
  - Studying magic in the capital
  - Traveling together
- Kestrel agrees.
- Night passes uneventfully.

────────────────────────
6) MORNING & GUILD MEETING
- Jayson receives a Guild summons (they already know about his sword).
- Party meets the Marshal and scribes.
- Jayson explains his actions in detail:
  - Use of fear as deterrence
  - Avoiding daylight massacres to prevent anti-beastkin rumors
- Guild accepts his explanation.
- Jayson formally plans:
  - Jobs and dungeons
  - Skill development
  - Capital-bound magic education
- Kestrel signs up for a plaque as well.

────────────────────────
7) MARKET, SHRINE & FLEAS
- Party visits the market and park.
- Jayson reminisces about his wives:
  - Ayaka, Seika, Naru, Varethyn, Shion, Lysera, Fei
- At a quiet shrine area, Jayson attempts to call Fleas:
  - A fox spirit kit that once lived in his fifth tail.
- Fleas reappears and reveals:
  - She can now speak (new development)
  - She remains bound to Jayson’s fifth tail
- Playful physical scuffle ensues (as per their history).
- Agreement reached:
  - Fleas may hide or appear at will
  - Must acknowledge Jayson when called
- Fleas vanishes back into the tail.

────────────────────────
8) KESTREL’S SHOULDER
- Jayson notices Kestrel’s shoulder injury predates the cave incident.
- Diagnoses slipped tendon manually.
- Performs a painful but effective physical adjustment.
- Explains reliance on magic in this world vs. physical healing.
- Shoulder stabilized; soreness expected but functional.

────────────────────────
9) LOWER MARKET INN — BAD PART OF TOWN
- Party enters an inn unknowingly located in a hostile, anti-beastkin district.
- Hostility escalates through slurs and intimidation.
- Jayson deflects insults with humor and composure.
- Apple crumble becomes a focal point, diffusing tension.
- Three other beastkin adventurers (D–C rank) enter and laugh openly.
- Their joy triggers renewed hostility from humans.

────────────────────────
10) STREET CONFRONTATION
- A large bald human (likely mercenary/miner) confronts the beastkin.
- Jayson verbally intervenes and redirects aggression toward himself.
- He ensures the fight moves outside to protect the inn and Liri.
- Outside:
  - Jayson removes outerwear and weapon.
  - Displays controlled physical dominance via martial arts.
  - Redirects punch, knees attacker, palm-strikes him ~15 feet.
- Opponent concedes.
- Crowd disperses without guards or bloodshed.

────────────────────────
11) RESOLUTION
- Jayson invites the man back inside for a drink.
- Buys ale, speaks about ending cycles of generational hatred.
- Martial salute exchanged.
- Leaves the inn peacefully with Kestrel and Liri.

────────────────────────
12) EVENING REFLECTION & REST
- Winter rain returns as night falls.
- Party finds the Rainward Inn.
- Jayson books:
  - One basic room (for himself)
  - One superior room with bath and fireplace (for Kestrel & Liri)
- He jokes about room assignments and retires early.
- Alone, Jayson reflects on:
  - The rain
  - Varethyn and the others
  - His immaturity when he held godhood
- He places the carved stone (spiral + portraits of his wives) on the windowsill.
- Ends the day acknowledging loss, growth, and restraint.
